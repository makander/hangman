Welcome to hangman

When you press the Start button the game will pick a random word from the category of household appliances. A number of empty boxes that match the length of the word will appear. You have to guess the word by pressing on the letter butons below. If you guess correctly the letter will appear in one of the boxes. If you're wrong you'll hang.
